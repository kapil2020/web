About Me

I am a researcher in travel behaviour, air-quality exposure, and sustainable urban mobility, currently completing my PhD at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur under the guidance of Prof. Arkopal Kishore Goswami. My interdisciplinary work bridges transportation engineering, environmental analytics, and behavioural modelling to advance equitable and cleaner urban mobility systems.

My goal is to integrate data-driven decision frameworks with human-centred design to address the twin challenges of urban pollution and mobility inequity. To this end, I have developed tools and models‚Äîranging from latent class choice models and machine learning frameworks to AI-enabled routing applications‚Äîthat quantify behavioural responses to air quality, travel information, and infrastructure design.

In my doctoral research, I led the development of DRUM (Dynamic Route Planning for Urban Mobility), a web-based routing tool that recommends pollution- and energy-optimized travel paths in real time. I also proposed the Perceived Walkability Score (PWS) framework, integrating computer vision and stated preference modelling to map urban walkability at neighbourhood scale. These contributions have been featured in The Hindu and recognized through multiple academic awards and grants, including a research exchange fellowship at the University of Leeds.

Prior to my PhD, I completed an M.Tech in Transportation Engineering from IIT Roorkee and a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from Rajasthan Technical University. I also gained professional experience at the World Resources Institute (WRI) India, studying in-vehicle exposure and commuter health risks across Indian cities.

I serve as a peer reviewer for leading journals including Transport Policy, Transportation Research: Part A, TRR, and RTBM, and am an active member of ASCE T&DI, Transportation Research Group of India, and WCTR Society. My long-term vision is to design intelligent, sustainable, and equitable mobility systems by combining behavioural insights, AI, and policy innovation.
